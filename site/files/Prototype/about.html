Why do we love VW Campers?

Welcome

Elizabeth and William Carey, have prepared every detail to ensure that your retro-chic vacation is a special event that you will long remember. Your experience starts on the road in their beautiful hippy-vehicle, there's no better set of wheels for exploring the sights of the New Forest in Hampshire.

They have looked after Florence and Geoffrey for over ten years and make sure they are sparkling to reflect your holiday and their personality. They are in the heart of the New forest, a magical place to be on the road.

Florence and Geoffrey, it must be said, are head-turners. People regularly stop in the in the New forest traffic to grin goofily, admire their stylish good looks, and even snap photos. they make you feel pretty cool.
